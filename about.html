a little bit about me...

hey there! i'm a third year at uc berkeley studying electrical engineering and computer science with a certificate in visual/interactive design. as a software developer interested in artistic applications, beautiful interfaces, and aesthetic user experiences, i have found a deep passion for UI/UX, product, and interactive design.


after working at facebook and designing, developing, and implementing my own mobile app, i took a liking towards iOS mobile development, ceating end-to-end user interfaces, and supplementary data analytics - creating appealing data visualizations is my specialty. i've dellivered several mockups and ideations of products to happy clients, and my background in software development has helped me to not only make these mockups visually pleasing, but easily implementable.


in addition to product, UI/UX and mobile design, i love to draw! a majority of my works include digital black and white line art and tattoo design commissions. i'm a huge fan of japanese-style wave art, and my favorite painting is the great wave of kanagawa (you may see small references to it throughout my portfolio). i'm also very passionate about sneakers and fashion - the subject of some of my most recent projects.


i'm always looking for opportunities to design and implement unique user products and experiences - please reach out if you think this would be a good fit!

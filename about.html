Hey there, I'm Pranathi!

I'm a junior at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering & Computer Science with a certificate in design.

For as long as I can remember, I've loved art and design - UX/product design has allowed me to combine my skills in tech with those in illustration/design to produce beautiful end-to-end interfaces. I take a specific liking for mobile application design, and my background in tech has given me the unique ability to not only mock up designs for aesthetic and functional applications, but to also create functional prototypes and implementatitons. At Cal, I am part of the executive committee of several student groups that helped build my skills as a designer, developer, and mentor.

In between school years, I interned at Blue Cheetah Analog Design, a startup based out of SF, and most recently, I was a University Engineering Intern at Facebook. I will be returning to Facebook as a SWE Intern in New York for Summer 2021.

When I'm not drawing, designing, or coding, I'm playing with my lab-shepherd mix Nora, or shopping for cool sneakers. :)
